Install

From your site

To install this app, login to your site and click on "Installer". Search for ERPNext Community Portal and click on "Install"

Using Bench

Go to your bench folder and setup the new app

$ bench get-app community_erpnext_com https://github.com/frappe/community_erpnext_com
$ bench new-site testsite
$ bench --site testsite install-app community_erpnext_com

Login to your site to configure the app.
